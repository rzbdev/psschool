Welcome to Pathseekers

Pathseekers is a community that celebrates the uniqueness of each child and gives them an enabling environment where they can discover their own abilities and develop as confident individuals. At Pathseekers, a child can seek his or her own path to success.

We at Pathseekers, value the creativity and potential hidden in each child and try to nurture it in a secure environment. We emphasize on helping them evolve as good human beings and achieve academic excellence. We encourage our children to develop self-discipline and respect the freedom and rights of others.

Pathseekers is run by the RSSB Educational & Environmental Society. It offers a high standard of education to students through 18 months to 14 plus years of age. Spread over an area of 37.57 Acres. Presently the school comprises of the Pre-School. Primary School and Middle School sections. The current strength is over 800 students across various classes. The school is fortunate to be situated amidst magnificent natural environment.

The school has four main buildings, all well-equipped with a spacious Gymnasium, Kitchen, Sports fields, Playgrounds, 2 Computer Labs, well-stocked Library, Language Laboratory, Logico - Mathematical Laboratory, Robotics Laboratory, Social Science, Math Laboratory, separate Composite Laboratory, Art areas and Free play areas.

The school follows an inquiry based, developmentally appropriate, play based curriculum. The Project Approach to learning has been integrated into the curriculum across classes allowing the child to explore knowledge and build experiences. Teachers work in teams, across all the age groups from infancy, toddlerhood, primary and middle and also ensure continuity in curriculum areas.

Our Vision at Pathseekers is to provide a secure and enabling environment that supports the creation of
Good Human Beings

Through the collaborative efforts of children, parents, teachers, and community working together as a team...

  • To provide a secure and challenging environment that fosters tolerance and mutual respect
  • To awaken an attitude of enquiry and enthusiasm for learning
  • To enable our children to be socially competent, emotionally mature, constructive decision makers, creative thinkers and above all, good human beings
