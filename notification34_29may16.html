Notification for Recruitment

34 28.05.2016

Applications are invited for CBSE SCHOOL at Dera Baba Jaimal Singh, District Amritsar, Punjab for undernoted Teaching Posts in Primary, Middle, Secondary Sections and Non-Teaching Post.

Note: Candidates applying should have

  • 1. Age between 20 years to 45 years
  • 2. Should be vegetarian and teetotaller

qualification and experience required

(A) Pre-Primary Section

1. Special Educator
  • i)  Graduate/Diploma in Special Education.
  •                               OR
  • B.Sc. in Child/ Human Development with Certificate in Special Education.
  • ii) English medium in Senior Secondary and Graduation with minimum 50% marks.
  • iii) Three Years of teaching experience from Recognized School / Organization.

(B) Primary Section

1. Dance & Drama Teacher
  • i)  Graduate with Performing Arts (Dance).
  •                               OR
  • Graduate in any discipline with three years experience in Theatre/Dance from Recognized Institute/Organization.
  • ii) Senior Secondary and Graduation with minimum 50% Marks.
  • iii) Three Years of teaching / training experience from Recognized School / Organization.

(C) Middle Section

1. Health Wellness Teacher (Counsellor)
  • i)   Post Graduate with Psychology and Counselling as a main subject from recognized University / College
  •                               OR
  • Graduate with Diploma in Guidance and Counselling from recognized University / College
  • ii) English medium in Senior Secondary, Graduation and Post-Graduation with minimum 50% marks
  • iii) Three Years of experience of Guiding and Counselling children in Recognized School / Organization
  • (Candidates with 60% and above marks in Senior Secondary, Graduation and B.Ed. and /or Post Graduates will be preferred)
2. Social Science Teacher
  • i)   a. i) Graduate with History / Political Science / Economics / Sociology / Geography as main subject from recognized University / College
  •      b. B.Ed. with Social Science (History / Political Science / Economics / Sociology / Geography) & any one of the other subjects from recognized University / College
  •                               OR
  • B.A. Ed. with Social Sciences of the Regional College of Education
  • ii) English medium in Senior Secondary, Graduation and B.Ed. with minimum 50% marks
  • iii) Three Years of teaching experience from Recognized School / Organization
  • iv) Preferably the candidate should have qualified Central Teacher Eligibility Test (CTET) conducted by CBSE on behalf of Central Government or Teacher Eligibility Test (TET) conducted by State Government Paper -II.
  • (Candidates with 60% and above marks in Senior Secondary, Graduation and B.Ed. and / or Post Graduates will be preferred)

(E) Non-Teaching

1. IT Manager
  • i) Masters in Computer Science / Computer Application / IT with at least 5 years experience in Systems and Network management and Software Development preferably in a School or Educational Institute.
  •                               OR
  •   Bachelor in Computer Science / Computer Application / IT with at least 7 years of experience in Systems and Network management and Software Development preferably in a School or Educational Institute.
  • ii) Certification in Server Infrastructure (MCSE: Solution Expert or Network) in Microsoft Technologies would be preferred.
Please note and ensure that:-
  • i) Experience of the related post from School/Organization, Recognized / Affiliated by some Board / Government, will be taken into consideration. Only this experience should be mentioned in the Application Form.
  • ii) School details mentioned on the Letter Head of experience certificate must specify Recognition/ Affiliation. If not, must be supported by a letter from school stating;
    • (a) Recognition / Affiliation No.
    • (b) Date of Recognition / Affiliation with name of the Board / Government
    • (c) Valid upto Class / Year.
  • iii) Regarding B.Ed. qualification, the TWO main teaching subjects qualified in B.Ed. must be mentioned in the Application form.
  • iv) The employment is on Probation cum-Contract basis for a period of ONE Year. After the successful completion of probation period of one year, the Teaching Staff will be confirmed and recruited on Regular basis, whereas the Non-Teaching Staff will be recruited on 3 years contract, which will be renewed after every 3 years.
  • v) The nomenclature of the 'Post Applied for' must be selected carefully in the Application form.
  • vi) The application can be submitted online at the website: http://careers.pathseekers.education. Applications will not be accepted via email.
  • vii) Last date for receiving applications: 15.06.2016.
  • viii) In the absence of any of the information as required above or incomplete form or given incorrect Application form, the Application form will be rejected.

Note: Eligible Candidates will have to go through Telephonic Interview, Shortlisted Candidates will be called for Written Test and Lesson Demonstration / Technical Competency Test and then Qualified Candidates will appear for Personal Interview. Cost of travel to Beas and other Incidentals will have to be borne by the Candidates.
